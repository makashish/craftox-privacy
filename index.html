Account Deletion - Craftox

At Craftox, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your account and data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Craftox account and all associated data at any time.

To delete your account, please send an email to:

matrusewa@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if available)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What happens after request?

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion.

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 3–7 working days.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Account information
  • User-generated content
  • App usage data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained if required for legal or security purposes.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the email above.

This process ensures complete and permanent deletion of your account and associated data.

If your account was created using Google Sign-In or any third-party service, please mention it in your email.

Children's Privacy

Craftox is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove the information.

Users must be at least 13 years old to use this application.
